Combating The Soil Carbon Crisis

Agric-Empower addresses one of the most urgent environmental challenges of our time - the rapid loss of soil carbon. Through regenerative agriculture and community education, we're restoring degraded soils and fighting climate change in Rhino Refugee Camp.

Our Climate Mission

Addressing the soil carbon crisis through regenerative agriculture and community empowerment

Our Mission

To combat the soil carbon crisis by empowering refugee farmers with regenerative agriculture knowledge and practices that restore soil health, sequester atmospheric carbon, and build climate-resilient food systems while improving livelihoods in Rhino Refugee Camp.

Our Vision

A world where degraded agricultural lands are restored to health, where farmers are climate champions sequestering carbon in their soils, and where refugee communities lead the global fight against the soil carbon crisis through regenerative agriculture.

Our Values

  • Climate Action: Urgently addressing soil carbon loss
  • Regeneration: Healing degraded agricultural lands
  • Community: Collective action for environmental restoration
  • Education: Understanding the soil carbon crisis
  • Resilience: Building climate-adaptive farming systems

How We Address The Soil Carbon Crisis

Comprehensive regenerative agriculture solutions to restore soil health and combat climate change

Soil Carbon Assessment

Community-based monitoring of soil organic carbon levels, tracking the restoration progress of degraded soils, and measuring the climate impact of regenerative farming practices through simple, accessible methods.

Carbon Sequestration Practices

Teaching farmers regenerative techniques like composting, cover cropping, agroforestry, and no-till farming that actively capture atmospheric carbon and store it in soil organic matter.

Climate-Smart Water Management

Water conservation strategies that support soil carbon restoration, including mulching for moisture retention and irrigation practices that enhance soil microbial activity.

Climate Crisis Education

Comprehensive training on the soil carbon crisis, climate change science, and regenerative agriculture practices that empower farmers to become environmental stewards.

Climate Action Community

Building a network of climate-conscious farmers who share knowledge, resources, and best practices for soil carbon restoration and environmental healing.
